Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hire India free for job seekers? +
Yes, completely free! Job seekers can create a profile, browse jobs, apply, save listings, and track applications at no cost. We only charge employers for posting jobs and premium features.
How do I apply for a job? +
Simply create a free account, complete your profile, then click "Apply Now" on any job listing. Your profile details are sent directly to the employer. You can track all applications in your dashboard.
How do I post a job as an employer? +
Register as an Employer from the signup page, then navigate to "Post a Job" in your dashboard. Choose a plan that fits your hiring needs — we offer both pay-per-post and subscription options.
How long does it take to hear back from employers? +
Response times vary by company. On average, candidates on Hire India hear back within 5 business days. Make sure your profile is complete to improve your chances of a quick response.
Is my personal data safe? +
Absolutely. We are fully GDPR and Indian data protection compliant. Your data is encrypted, never sold to third parties, and you have full control over your privacy settings at any time.
